Why this story matters

The same month Roe v Wade was overturned in 2022, I was drugged at a gay club in West Hollywood, woke up in a hospital with no memory, bruised knees and a $1,724.89 medical bill only for the LAPD to never get back to me.

This story is my revenge.

– Lily Malm, Screenwriter
